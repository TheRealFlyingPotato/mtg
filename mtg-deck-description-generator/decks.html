Approach of the Sunbird


The point of this deck is to cast Sunbird's Invocation and then Approach of the Second Sun, thus winning you the game. Get lots of mana with a pile of mana rocks, getting you to the spells listed above or, if you can't hit those, a couple of Ulamog's Crushers should be enough to end your opponent's misery as well.

Barely Blue Myr Mirrorworks Combo


Possibly the most convuluted deck on this page, this beautiful deck that has almost no color in it shines bright with its Myr. Mirrorworks is the most fun card in this deck. The rest of the combo consists of Myr Galvanizer, Palladium Myr, Thermal Navigator, and Workshop Assistant. There's another combo that uses Powerstone Shard. Not going to explain everything on here, if you can figure it out, good for you!

Conduit Sagas


This deck focuses on using Power Conduit along with Chainer's Torment to repeatedly drain life from your opponents. The Eldest Reborn is another way for Power Conduit to be abused, while cards such as Ill-Gotten Inheritance, Defiant Bloodlord, and Agent of Masks assist in the draining.

Dictate Pingers


Warning: This deck could be very bad but also hilarious. Aiming to play cards that tap to deal damage, such as Goblin Fireslinger and Vent Sentinel, defend with walls for Vent Sentinel such as Fog Bank, and play Dictate of the Twin Gods and pray that your damage output can beat your opponents.

Dimir Amassing Wizards


Wizards are a meddling bunch, and you love them. Get out your star player, Naban, Dean of Iteration, and then throw down Aether Adept and Exclusion Mage to clear the way for your Gleaming Overseer and Vizier of the Scorpion's abomination. Riverwise Augur, Pondering Mage, and Azami, Lady of Scrolls keep your hand filled with cards while your opponents weep at your power.

Dimir Ninjas


Become one with your stealthy side and play the ninja deck at the table. Slither Blade or Ornithopter on turn one, then Ingenious Infiltrator the next turn to start drawing cards. Fallen Shinobi to... borrow... your opponent's cards, then Ninja of the New Moon to deal massive damage.

Flicker Drake


This combo / light control deck seeks to combo off with Peregrine Drake, Ghostly Flicker, and Archaeomancer. These three generate infinte mana, allowing you to draw your deck with Sea Gate Oracle, the make everyone else lose with Blue Sun's Zenith.

Gates


A 5-color battle deck? Yessir! This deck uses 22 guildgates (such as Azorius Guildgate) and takes advantage of them with Gatebreaker Ram, Gate Colossus, Guild Summit, and Archway Angel.

Golgari Desecrated Tomb


What? Desecrated Tomb. How? Reassembling Skeleton and Bloodsoaked Champion as the conduits, Culling Dais and Bloodflow Connoisseur to keep doing it. Make a bunch of bats, swing in for lots of damage. If you need more lands or more creatures, Evolutionary Leap and Perilous Forays are your friends.

Golgari Sherei


This deck focuses around using Shirei, Shizo's Caretaker to generate lots of value with cards like Sakura-Tribe Elder, Deep Forest Hermit, and Elvish Visionary. Bringing these cards back over and over with Sherei's ability and sacrificing to themselves or to Bloodthrone Vampire allows for an engine that's bound to take over the game. Also, Fecundity because Fecundity.

Izzet Weenies


This deck revolves around the card Cavalcade of Calamity. Play a bunch of small dispensable creatures (Dragon Fodder, Goblin Gathering) or hard to block boys (Ornithopter, Slither Blade) and swing in.

Midnight Gond Combo


Do you like tokens? How about a lot of tokens? This deck aims to make all the tokens you can dream of with Presence of Gond attached to Midnight Guard. Use Commune with the Gods to get there and Soul Warden to get loads of life from your tokens hitting the field.

Mono-Blue Mystic Forge


This artifact deck makes use of M20's Mystic Forge. Play this, Reduce artifact costs with Etherium Sculptor, Make a bunch of tokens with Efficient Construction and Golem Foundry, gain a bunch of life with Golem's Heart, then do it all again with an Elixir of Immortality.

Mono-White Soul Sisters


At the heart of this deck lies Soul Warden with a helping hand in Ajani's Welcome and Anointer Priest. Play a bunch of small creatures and tokens with Battle Screech, Raise the Alarm, and Doomed Traveler, gain a lot of life with Angel of Vitality, draw cards with Mentor of the Meek, beat face. The end.

Saproling Swarm


Time to chuck out a bunch of 1/1s with this Saproling deck. Early game, use Saproling Migration and Yavimaya Sapherd to get started. Later, hope for Arcane Melee and Memory Crystal to let you cast Whispers of the Muse and Sprout Swarm over and over and over, running over your opponent with an army of small plants.

Selesnya Enchantress


Out-value your oppoenents in this Enchantress deck. With 8 Satyr Enchanter type effects, you're bound to hit your Starfield Mystic and Sigil of the Empty Throne to start pumping out more angels than you can count.

Simic Ascendancy


Opponent seems to have a million life? Or maybe they really do? Who cares! Simic Ascendancy sure doesn't. Play cards that give themselves counters like Chronomaton and Heroes' Bane, ramp with New Horizons, and hope you gret the copy of Wilderness Reclamation to end the game without ever touching your opponent's life total.

Simic Superfriends


You heard me right - thanks to War of the Spark, 10$ superfriends is a thing. Jiang Yanggu, Wildcrafter, Vraska, Swarm's Eminence, Arlinn, Voice of the Pack, and (if you're lucky) Nissa, Voice of Zendikar do some work. Proliferate effects such as Thrummingbird keep your Planeswalkers going and Mowu, Loyal Companion just might be enough to topple your opponent's life total.
